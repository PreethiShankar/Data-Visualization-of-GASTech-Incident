Visual Investigation of VAST Mini Challenge part 1

Visual analytics plays a vital role in investigations, as it allows investigators to identify patterns, trends, and anomalies in large amounts of data. By using visual analytics tools, investigators can quickly and easily spot important clues that might otherwise go unnoticed. For example, they can create visualizations that help them understand the relationships between individuals, organizations, or events. They can also use these tools to explore and analyze data from multiple sources, such as financial records, social media activity, and surveillance footage. By combining their expertise with powerful visual analytics tools, investigators can more effectively identify and solve crimes, gather evidence.

Welcome to the home page of website on VAST Challenge Mini Challenge 1. This challenge is attempted as a part of the coursework of CSE 578: Data Visualization (2023 Spring), taught by Professor Dr. Chris Bryan at Arizona State University.
